Assalāmu ʿalaikum

I’m currently a Research Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maine, where I explore the intersections of AI, security, and embedded systems.

After completing my B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (2011) and M.Tech. in Software Engineering (2013) with honors, I joined the Department of Computer Engineering at ZHCET, Aligarh Muslim University.

I’ve taught a wide range of core computer science subjects and supervised student projects on Android app development, machine learning, networking, and image processing.

My current research focuses on enhancing the reliability and security of Large Language Models (LLMs), with broader interests in computer vision and multimodal human activity recognition.

Outside the lab, I’m a passionate photographer—capturing fleeting moments and serene landscapes. Explore my work on my photography portfolio.
